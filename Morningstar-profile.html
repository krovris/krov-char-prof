BIOGRAPHY
Morningstar is a young adult who has recently taken over District 74 as mayor after beating the old mayor to death with a morning star. Before she took such drastic action, she was known for organizing volunteer 'neighborhood watch' patrols to keep her local streets safe. This, along with her charismatic personality has, over time, swayed many people to her side. However in recent years she's had a habit of being rude, impatient and violent in response to provocation. Some believe it is because she's mourning a friend... Many unfamiliar with Star believe her to be a brainless thug hell-bent on violent revenge for the current state of 74 and for the murder of her entire family, but her mission is much more than that. She, like many of wants to lead a revolution against the city, and she's not alone. Star has gained contacts and connections with criminals outside the blockade who have been all too willing to help her in her mission-- if only to destabilize the city in the short-term.
