About

Tessa Hackett wants to make a name for herself as a villain, but being able to punch people into submission is not exactly enough when it comes to villainy. And to be honest, her stamina sucks. She has Asthma that can also be described as COPD, and no, its not good. She runs out of breath quickly and has to have her inhaler on hand. Her gauntlets weigh 30+ pounds, and her boots allow her to jump wildly far distances. As a supervillain, she's firmly chaotic evil. She wants to ruin the Superheroes' days, stick out as a bad guy, and become the Next Big Thing to fear. Unfortunately, the more attention she garners, the less people seem to take her seriously. Havin a "Danny Phantom" magical girl transformation is a part of it.
