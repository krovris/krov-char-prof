ABOUT

Eight is an enigmatic criminal known for its surgical precision and penchant for creating horrific bio-experiments on its unwilling victims. It lives in the heart of Industrial District #1000, long considered unsafe for people due to biochemical contamination, which yes, Eight had created. As far as the City knows, the district is uninhabitable. It has taken over an abandoned residential block and uses this location as its main base of operations. It is skilled in hacking into The City's intranet and has implants to help facilitate its access. Its body is not the one it was born in-- rather a third version of a project that has spanned over 80 years. Whether or not Eight is immortal is irrelevant because it can prolong its own life using its knowledge of cellular structure and DNA encoding. It has killed many people. It plans to kill many, many more.
