the superior void

the largest, emptiest intra-galactal space ever discovered.

its thought the superior void formed after a bunch of stars went supernova billions and billions of years ago. just like its inferior sibling, its bathed in weird disruptive fields that make navigation within it difficult.

there are a host of planets and stars that have been colonized since the start of the usurper / eusebia prime merger, but the lack of conventional travel has limited immigration.

the inferior void

the second-largest void within usurper and a royal pain in the ass.

this region of space is two times smaller than its superior sibling. its considered a dangerous anomoly to most interstellar travellers. many shipfulls of people have gone missing here, and few who enter return.

veyl

a rogue planet of rock and ice drifting aimlessly in the dark.

with no solar system, this planet floats around within the inferior void with no direction. despite being a frozen hellscape that lacks an atmosphere, there has been reports of a group of people living there, studying the strange phenomena the inferior void has to offer

this is lev's home planet.
